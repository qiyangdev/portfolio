I build across Apple platforms, mobile, and the web — and each surface asks for
a different kind of craft.

SwiftUI is where I shape native experiences on Apple devices. Expo keeps mobile
iteration fast without losing reach. Next.js is my default when the product
needs to live on the web.

The goal is not to collect frameworks. It is to pick the right surface, ship
something useful, and keep learning along the way.

```ts
const stack = ["SwiftUI", "Expo", "Next.js"] as const;
```
